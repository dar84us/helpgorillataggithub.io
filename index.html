How to Help

What is #HelpGorillaTag?

Gorilla Tag is an iconic VR game that has brought millions of players together. The community is now rallying under #HelpGorillaTag to show support, raise awareness, and celebrate the game’s impact.

My Story

I personally never had the chance to play Gorilla Tag, but I have seen how much joy, laughter, and community it has brought to VR players around the world. Watching clips and hearing stories from fans showed me how important this game is to so many people. That’s why I want to help spread the word and keep its legacy alive through #HelpGorillaTag.

How You Can Help

  • Use #HelpGorillaTag on TikTok, Twitter (X), and YouTube.
  • Share your favorite Gorilla Tag memories online.
  • Support the developers and community groups.
  • Invite friends to join the movement.
